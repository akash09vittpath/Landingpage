ABOUT US

Excellent Services

Bombay Tools Centre is the place where we find tools and industrial solutions that make work easy.

Our Organisation was established in 1993 in Mumbai, and we have over 30 years of experience in the cutt ing tools business.

We run from Nagdevi Street, one of India’s oldest and largest industrial markets. Under the guidance of Mr Harsh Shah, the company has built a solid reputation for dependable supply, ethical business, and consistent quality.

Bombay Tools Centre is one of India’s most trusted names in cutting tools, CNC/VMC tooling systems, and precision instruments.

Bombay Tools Centre offers a wide variety of cutting tools designed to meet the requir ements of industries such as manufacturing, oil & gas, automotive, engineering, and more.

We provide quality tools that help you work smarter, faster, and safer.
